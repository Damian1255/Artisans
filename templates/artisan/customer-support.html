Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the Account button at the top right corner of our homepage and follow the prompts to provide your information.

How can I browse and purchase artworks?

Simply navigate to our homepage and explore a wide selection of artworks. Once you find one you love, click on it to view details and follow the instructions to purchase.

How can I request a custom artwork?

To request a custom artwork tailored to your preferences, visit our "Request Custom Art" page and fill out the form with your requirements. Our artists will then get in touch with you in 3-5 business days to discuss the details.

Can I sell my own artwork on your website?

Absolutely! We provide a platform for artists to sell their own artwork. Visit our "Sell Your Art" page to learn more about the process and upload your artworks for sale.

How does the art workshop registration work?

To participate in our art workshops, browse our available workshops on the "Art Workshops" page and click on the one you're interested in. Follow the instructions to register and secure your spot.

What are the payment options available on your website?

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal. During the checkout process, you can select your preferred payment option.

How long does it take to process a refund?

Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days after the request is approved. The exact time may vary depending on your payment method and financial institution.

How can I track the status of my order?

Once your order is confirmed and shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track the status of your shipment on the respective courier's website.

What is your return policy?

We have a flexible return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can initiate a return within 30 days of receiving the artwork. Please refer to our "Refunds" page for detailed instructions.

How can I contact your customer support team for further assistance?

For any additional questions or concerns, you can reach out to us by filling out the support form at the bottom of this webpage. Alternatively, you may email us at artisans@gmail.com for non-urgent matters. Lastly, call us at +65 1234 5678 for urgent matters.

What are the shipping options available for artworks?

We offer various shipping options, including standard and express shipping. The available options and associated costs will be displayed during the checkout process.

Can I cancel or modify my order after it has been placed?

Once an order is placed, we strive to process and ship it as quickly as possible. Therefore, we recommend contacting our customer support team immediately if you need to cancel or modify your order, and we will assist you to the best of our ability.

Can I return or exchange a custom artwork?

Due to the personalized nature of custom artworks, returns or exchanges are typically not accepted unless there is a defect or error in the artwork. We encourage you to communicate your requirements clearly during the custom artwork request process to ensure your satisfaction.
